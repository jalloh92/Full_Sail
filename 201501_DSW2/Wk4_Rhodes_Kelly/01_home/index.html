TEAZER's Back for One Night Only!

Friday, February 20th at Wills Pub

17 Years and 3 Epic Gigs

Few have heard of TEAZER, and for those who have, the sweet memories of their epic gigs are a faint memory. In 1998, the glam rock band TEAZER was formed, and music was forever changed.

TEAZER is reuniting, for one night only, at this year's Ralphfest. Be there on Friday night, February 20th at Will's Pub, to witness a performance you will never forget.
