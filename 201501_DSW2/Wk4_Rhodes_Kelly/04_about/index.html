Band Bio

Juan. Oliver. Phillup. Holden. These are the names of Norwegian rock royalty. TEAZER is the world's most infamous rock band, known for their excess and antics.

The members of TEAZER met in their teens at reform school in Oslo, Norway. They discoverd a mutual love of heavy rock, young girls, and black eyeliner. Juan, Oliver, Phillup and Holden knew at a young age that they were destined for fame. TEAZER was formed in 1998.

At the pinnacle of their notoriety, they performed to a sold-out crowd at the Peacock Room in Orlando, FL in 2006. Outrageous hair, bad fashion and on-stage antics marked the performance. A show of that magnitude has not been seen in Central Florida since then.

However, this fame was fleeting. TEAZER faced exinction when drummer Holden Cheex was supposedly kidnapped by "teenage Russian girls" immediately after their gig at the Peacock Room. In early 2015, Holden was released from captivity and the band reunited. TEAZER plans to not only recapture the momentum they had achieved in 2006, but catapult to world-wide dominance.

We are:

  • Juan Ascru: Vocals and Lead Guitar
  • Oliver Clothsov: Lead Guitar and Vocals
  • Phillup Yoraz: Vocals and Bass Guitar
  • Holden Cheex: Vocals and Drums
